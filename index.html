Trust Every File. Trace Every Hash.

Blockchain-powered authenticity & provenance system for a transparent digital world.

Background

GAB (Global Authentication Blockchain) addresses challenges like greenwashing, fake sustainability claims, and unverifiable reports by enabling traceable, blockchain-backed provenance for files and documents.

Objective

To leverage blockchain for secure and scalable verification, allowing anyone to trace and prove the authenticity of data, research outputs, or ESG reports with a single QR code or hash reference.
